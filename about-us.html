We started Lune because most clothing asks too much of you.

Too much money for too little wear. Too many trends for too little thought. We set out to make the opposite: pieces that feel obvious once you have them, and that you reach for again and again without deciding to.

Founded in 2019 in Mumbai, Lune is a small team with a single-minded focus on the basics done right — not basic as in boring, but basic as in foundational. The wardrobe that everything else orbits around.

Fabric first. Everything follows from there.

We spend most of our time finding the right materials. Linens that breathe. Wools that don't scratch. Cottons that hold their colour through a hundred washes. We work directly with mills in Gujarat and Tamil Nadu, choosing substance over novelty at every step.

No seasonal collections. No artificial scarcity. We restock what sells and retire what doesn't. Simple as that.
